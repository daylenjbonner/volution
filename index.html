    Volution #

    Volution is currently being developed by myself for fun. The hope is that through the dialog between Agent and Ground, a novel way of thinking can emerge to aid the creative exploration of virtually any question you might have. Assuming, of course, that sense can be made of my peculiar web of interpretations.

    ..as a dialectic #

    As mentioned above, volution is conscidered a dialog between Agent and Ground. Like any good dialog, each participant is both accepting and rejecting of the others ideas. The edge of agreement and disagreement incites creative sparks.

    ..as a wiki #

    Yes, this wiki is indeed considered a component of volution. Serving as a quick reference what volution is about.

    ..as a paradigm #

    One way to view volution is as a paradigm that inherits from, at minimum, the neo-Newtonian, neo-Darwinian, neo-Jungian, and neo-Hegelian schools of thought. Inspiration is drawn from an array of well-tested explanations ranging from how galaxies form to how particles behave when entangled.

    ..as a system #

    Another view is as a system for generating conjectures about grounded agents.

    ..as a framework #

    As a collection of models that can modulate each other within a web of dependencies.

    ..as a language #

    Talking in volution is possible once grokked. May be used to describe your life from particle to multiverse.

    ..as a simulator #

    Could be viewed as a way to simulate agents without a ground or potentially as an engine for simulators in general.
